						drop into their matching slot on a condition. Drag "Condition" or "Group ( )" into a

						condition or group to negate it. Boolean fields get a fixed True/False toggle instead
						of a typed value — e.g. to say "not hasFile", drop the hasFile field and choose False
						(or use the NOT badge on the condition instead).
